Our Heritage

A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in 2000, Yulongfu was born from a deep love for the art of Chinese dumpling making. What began as a small family kitchen in Melbourne's Chinatown has blossomed into one of the city's most cherished culinary destinations.

Every bun is handcrafted using techniques passed down through generations, with ingredients sourced fresh from local Melbourne markets. Our master chefs work before dawn to ensure each dumpling meets the highest standard of excellence.
